Your data is our responsibility

Classbees takes data protection and data privacy seriously. All data are encrypted at rest and in transit and are in line with the UK&I data protection law.


Classbees application is on a cloud-based secured server located in the UK&I data centre and its data is stored and backed up frequently, so it can never be lost. Only authorised users will have access to the account and all passwords are stored as one way hash.


Classbees have monitoring in place to detect any potential breaches in accordance with our obligations. In an event, a robust processes and procedures are in place to control and limit any compromise. We only the latest encryption methods for all data transfer and policy ensures it is both tested and updated as per the cipher standards requirement.
